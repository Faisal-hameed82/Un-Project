UN Theme Week 2019

Walk the Talk

The UN Student Association invites to a week with concerts, talent show, seminar, debates, innovation workshops and more. UN Theme Week 2019 will be held from the 3rd till the 9th of March 2019.

The UN Student Association Oslo are proud to organise and invite you to the second UN Theme Week 2019: Walk the Talk - the first festival of its kind to be arranged by a voluntary student organisation. Working alongside more than 20 collaborators during the festival, the events will certainly ensure a week filled with enticing debates, lectures, workshops, concerts and more. Taking place in the vibrant city of Oslo from the 3rd to the 9th of March, the week will be dedicated to shedding light on important global issues that affect every aspect of our society. We invite you to join us in the conversation and in the increasingly important exchange of ideas.

UN STUDENTS OSLO AT A GLANCE

Here at UN Student Association Oslo, we are driven by a single goal; to bring attention to important issues that impact our global society and ensure the preservation of the universal human rights for all. We strive to impact the world at large by creating a sustainable world in which there is international solidarity, peace and cooperation. As a student organisation, we primarily reach out to everyone enrolled in higher education in Oslo and its surrounding vicinity, though our events are open for all. Read more about how you can engage with the UN Student Association Oslo under 'About Us' or contact us directly for any queries. With our organisation’s mission always in mind, we constantly strive to find new strategies for reaching out in order to spread knowledge and awareness about current issues that impact the world. Contact us to learn more about our commitment to spreading awareness through our various events and programmes and find out how you can do the same.

Last year, the UN Student Association was proud to work with organisations such as Doctors Without Borders, Oslo Peace Centre, SPIRE, Oslo World 2017, Norwegian Institute of International Affairs and the United Nations Association of Norway to create events that tackled important issues. As an independent student organisation, UNSA Oslo work to highlight issues that are important to us. worldpeople1200x7171.png With these initiatives, our goal is to promote profound awareness around global issues . The ability to reach out to youths has an immense power to truly transform. Learn more about our work by getting in touch with our team today or read about our previous work.
